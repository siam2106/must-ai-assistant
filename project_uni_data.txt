# 1. Overview of the University

Mody University of Science and Technology is a private higher education institution located in Rajasthan, India. It was established in **1998 by Shri R.P. Mody**, an industrialist and philanthropist, with a vision to provide quality education in a modern academic environment.

The university is located on a large **residential campus of approximately 265 acres**, offering an ecosystem that integrates academics, research, and extracurricular development.

Historically, the university was established as a **women-focused institution**, aiming to empower women through higher education. In recent years, it has expanded its academic structure and programs while continuing to emphasize empowerment, discipline, and holistic development.

# SET_DEPT Table Documentation

# Table Name

**SET_DEPT**

## Purpose

The **SET_DEPT** table stores information about all academic courses offered by the **School of Engineering and Technology (SET)** at **Mody University of Science and Technology, Laxmangarh, Rajasthan**.

This table is dedicated exclusively to courses offered by the School of Engineering and Technology. It contains information about each course, its complete tuition fee, course duration, and the tuition fee applicable for each academic year.

**Important Note:**

Every fee stored in this table represents **Tuition Fees only**.

The fee values **do not include** any of the following:

* Hostel Fees
* Career Development Fees
* SEFS Charges
* Examination Fees
* Security Deposit
* Registration Charges
* Transportation Fees
* Library Charges
* Laboratory Charges
* Any other miscellaneous fee

The table strictly stores **academic tuition fees only**.

The table contains the following columns.

---

## 1. Name_of_the_Courses_SET

**Data Type:** VARCHAR

### Description

This column stores the official name of every academic course offered under the **School of Engineering and Technology (SET)**.

* BCA
* BCA (Cyberdojo)

2. Multiple teachers can belong to the same department.

4. Department names may be stored or queried using either their full names or abbreviations.

* "SET" = "School of Engineering and Technology"
* "SLAS" = "School of Liberal Arts and Sciences"
* "SOL" = "School of Law"
* "SOP" = "School of Pharmacy"
* "SOD" = "School of Designing"
* "SOB" = "School of Business"

6. Searches should be case-insensitive.

For example:

* set
* SET
* School of Engineering and Technology

should all return the same faculty members.
